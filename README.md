## Maritimes (NB, NS, PEI)

- [Skills For Hire Atlantic](https://skillsforhire.ca/) - Virtual upskilling in Data Analytics & AI or cyber security
- [Atlantic Tech Summit](https://www.atlantictechsummit.com/) - Annual Tech Summit
- [Data For Good Maritimes](https://maritimes.dataforgood.ca/) - Datathons for social good
- [TechImpact](https://www.techimpact.it/) - Organization focused on digital transformations in Atlantic Canada
- [Startup Atlantic](https://startupatlantic.ca/) - Startup hub with tech related events

## Nova Scotia

- [Halifax dbt Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/halifax-dbt-meetup/) - Halifax, Nova Scotia, Data meetup focused on dbt (data build tool), Halifax based but with virtual attendance option
- [Halihax](https://www.halihax.com/) - Halifax, Nova Scotia tech and data community and meetups
- [Digital Nova Scotia](https://digitalnovascotia.com/) -  Nova Scotia tech hub, events, upskilling, job board, job seeker resources and much more
- [Volta](https://www.voltaeffect.com/) - Halifax, Nova Scotia - AI startup incubator community and events


## New Brunswick

- [CollabHub Atlantic](https://collabhubatlantic.ca/) - New Brunswick tech hub - events, job board, upskilling, tech news
- [Ignite](https://myignite.ca/) - Fredericton, New Brunswick economic development, entrepreneurship, tech and tech adjacent events, job seeker tools
- [Civic Tech Fredericton](https://www.civictechfredericton.com/) - Fredericton, New Brunswick meetup group focused on addressing social challenges with technology 
- [Moncton Tech Hive](https://www.facebook.com/people/Moncton-Tech-Hive) - Moncton, New Brunswick  dev and tech enthusiast community hosting events
- [Civic Tech Moncton](https://civictechmoncton.org/) - Moncton, New Brunswick meetup group focused on addressing social challenges with technology 
- [Venn Innovation](https://www.venninnovation.com/) - Moncton, New Brunswick - NB tech entrepreneurship and innovation hub hosting a meeting space and meetups
- [Civic Tech Saint John](https://civictechsaintjohn.ca/) - Saint John, New Brunswick meetup group focused on addressing social challenges with technology 
- [ConnexionWorks](https://www.connexionworks.ca/) - Saint John, New Brunswick coworking and entrepreneurship space hosting meetups and events
- [Google Developers Group Saint John](https://www.linkedin.com/company/gdg-saint-john) - Saint John, New Brunswick 

## Prince Edward Island

- [Prince Edward Island IT Alliance](https://peiitalliance.com/) - PEI tech hub - events, dev groups, tech news
